BEHIND THE BRAND

Lavish Hijab is a business based on the fashion industry that offers hijab products, including bawal and shawl and customizable accessorized charms to the customers. Lavish Hijab strives to achieve its vision and mission as well as to surely serve the customers with the best quality of product that will serve them a sophisticated and classic finish to any hijab style. As we know, hijab is necessary to all Muslim woman, and nowadays hijab becomes one of the trends for woman to be more fashionable. Therefore, the concept of our business is to sell the hijab products to the customers which have high quality and affordable prices as well as to show the modernity of the fashion sense of women nowadays.

OUR LOGO

The meaning of the word lavish is sumptuously rich or luxurious. We do our best to incorporate lavish words into our product as our hijab product comes from high quality and the designs are stunning. Thus, we do not want to disappoint our customers or make them feel like being lied to upon seeing our quality and designs of our hijab. Meanwhile, the word Hijab represents the main product that Lavish Hijab served alongside fashion accessories like charms. Usually, our customers styled their beautiful and colourful casual hijabs in a variety of styles with ease. They may make their hijab look sophisticated and stylish by embellishing it.
